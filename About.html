App-Ark was founded by four college students who wanted to revolutionize the international education industry for Chinese high school applicants aiming to study abroad. After experiencing the consequences of “educational” agencies that lacked in professional and ethical credibility, App-Ark’s founders set out to provide a service that prioritizes students’ personal growth instead of fabricating results. Over the past several years, our team and services have expanded, reaching out to guide hundreds of students and their families.

Company Timeline

  • Company Beginnings

    2009

    Initially founded as an online individual consulting service. Established a 5m^2 physical office space in Xuhui

  • Foundation

    2010

    First class of 13 students enrolled in March. App-Ark's current core members Fei Ni, Jason Lu, and Yvonne Li joined team.

  • Internal Development

    2011

    Established marketing, administration, and finance departments. Partnered up with Fudan University students for mentoring. Hans Zhang, App-Ark's current Marketing Director, joined the team. Moved to 80m^2 office near Fudan.

  • Strategic Model Redesigned

    2012

    Shifted from a one-on-one service to a team-based structure to allow for collaboration in students' application process. Extended marketing to conferences, expanding to the Suzhou area. Moved to another office, now at 120m^2.

  • Employee Growth

    2013

    Hired more full-time employees, with the first group of U.S. Consultants invited to work in the Shanghai branch, broadening App-Ark's international scope. With more staff and students, a new office was needed, expanding to 250m^2.
